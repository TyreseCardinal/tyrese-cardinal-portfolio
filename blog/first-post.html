Finally Starting My Portfolio Site

October 21st, 2025

After many delays in starting my portfolio, out of fear of failure and perfectionism, I've finally decided to stop procrastinating and avoiding it and to just start and gain some momentum. I’ve been overthinking what to include, how it should look, and whether it’ll ever be good enough—but I realized it's not going to build itself, and YOLO.

What I Set Up

I created my project folder, built a simple homepage, and added a blog directory. It’s not flashy, but it works—and that’s all I care about right now.

What I Learned

Sometimes the hardest part isn’t the coding—it’s getting past the hesitation to begin. Plus, I'm bored of xbox and need to do something productive.

What’s Next

Depends where I finish tonight. Let's see where this 3AM surge of motivation takes me I guess.

Takeaway

Perfection is hilariously evil with how much time it can waste. It is a toxic mindset to have when trying to create anything, it doesn't exist in the real world, just do it. - Nike or - Shia Labeouf.
